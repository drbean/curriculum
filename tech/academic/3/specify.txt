English[[13]edit]

Etymology[[14]edit]

   From [15]Old French [16]specifier, [17]especefier

Pronunciation[[18]edit]

     * [19]IPA^([20]key): /ˈspɛsɪfaɪ/

   [21]Headset icon.svg This entry needs [22]audio files. If you have a

Verb[[26]edit]

   specify (third-person singular simple present [27]specifies, present
   participle [28]specifying, simple past and past participle
   [29]specified)
    1. ([30]transitive) To [31]state [32]explicitly, or in [33]detail, or
       as a [34]condition.
    2. ([35]transitive) To [36]include in a [37]specification.
    3. ([38]transitive) To [39]bring about a specific [40]result.
