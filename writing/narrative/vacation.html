50 words about your vacation

Look at the textbook at 

http://web.nuu.edu.tw/~greg/textbook.pdf on the 29th page of the PDF (page 7 of the book). It says:


Think (& write a note) 

1. What you planned to do 

2. What you did

3. What you look back at with satisfaction 

4. What you regret 

5. What you learned

What questions do you think other students will ask you about your vacation. Try to answer those questions before they ask them.
